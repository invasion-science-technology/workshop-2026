Session 4 — Opportunities in data collection and modelling

10:20–10:50 · Coffee break

Session 5 — AI in practice: short tech talks

  • Format: 2–3 focused talks (about 15–20 minutes each) plus an applied talk on invasive weed management, with brief Q&A

Talk 1

Foundations — modern AI for spatiotemporal ecological data

  • Architectures, pipelines, uncertainty, interpretability, deployment

Talk 2

Bio-oriented AI application

  • Case study with lessons generalisable to invasive weeds

Talk 3

Application of AI to invasive weed management

  • End-to-end example: data sources, model, validation, performance, management implications

Short panel Q&A · 15–20 min

Break (optional — coffee and emails)

Closing remarks

  • Thanks, recap, and how outputs will be shared (report, mailing list, etc.)
